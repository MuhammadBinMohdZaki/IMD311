My Dream Places to go

Anfield Stadium


I am a football fan and I have supported the Liverpool FC club for 
5 years. The football club has been an inspiration to me. If someday, 
I have the opportunity to go to Anfield Stadium, I cannot describe 
how satisfied I will be. I also hope to be able to cheer with other 
Liverpool FC supporters at the stadium at future football matches.
 

Italy is a developed country but rich in interesting history. 
I want to go there because the building design in their city 
is very unique and classic. Italy has very beautiful cities to 
visit. In addition, Italy is considered one of the "Food Paradise" 
countries. Food like classic pizza, all kinds of pasta, 
lasagna, tiramisu, cannoli, and bruschetta.

Italy

Makkah


As you know, Muslims are required to perform Hajj and Umrah if they 
are able. I am a follower of Islam, I know the duties that I have 
to perform if I am able. So, Makkah is definitely a destination on 
my list to visit one day. Hearing from the stories of people who 
have performed Hajj and Umrah, they say that the place is very calm 
and they can feel the blessings of life. They also say, there is no 
place that feels a different aura than Makkah. As followers of Islam, 
we already know that Makkah is the home of Allah SWT, so it is not 
surprising that the atmosphere there is very calm and great.
 

I live in the city of Manjung. When holiday, I often choose to spend 
my holidays at the beach for a variety of reasons, ranging from the 
desire for relaxation to the appeal of natural beauty and recreational 
opportunities. The beach provides a serene and tranquil environment, 
with the soothing sound of waves and the gentle rustle of palm trees 
creating a calming atmosphere. The sight of expansive ocean views and 
the feel of soft sand beneath one's feet contribute to a sense of 
escapism from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
